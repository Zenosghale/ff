Consumer behavior is the study of how individuals make decisions about purchasing goods and services. It involves analyzing factors such as preferences, income, culture, and social influences. Businesses study consumer behavior to understand what customers want. Therefore, it helps in creating effective marketing strategies.

Psychological factors such as motivation and perception influence buying decisions. Social factors like family and peer groups also play a role. Economic conditions can affect spending patterns. Therefore, consumer behavior is complex and varies among individuals.

Understanding consumer behavior helps businesses improve products and customer satisfaction. It also helps in targeting the right audience. Therefore, it is essential for successful marketing.
