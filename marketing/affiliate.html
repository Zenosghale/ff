Affiliate marketing is a type of performance-based marketing where individuals earn a commission by promoting products or services. Affiliates use websites, blogs, and social media platforms to advertise products. When a customer makes a purchase through their link, they receive a commission. Therefore, affiliate marketing is beneficial for both businesses and marketers.

It is a cost-effective strategy because businesses only pay for actual sales. Affiliates can earn income without creating their own products. Many online platforms support affiliate programs. Therefore, it has become a popular method of earning online.

However, success in affiliate marketing requires effort and consistency. Building trust with the audience is important. Therefore, it is a competitive but rewarding field.
