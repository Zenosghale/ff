Digital marketing refers to the promotion of products and services using digital technologies, mainly the internet. It includes various channels such as search engines, websites, email, and mobile apps. Businesses use digital marketing to reach a global audience quickly and efficiently. It is cost-effective compared to traditional marketing methods. Therefore, digital marketing has become a key strategy for modern businesses.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is an important part of digital marketing, helping websites rank higher in search results. Paid advertising, such as Google Ads, allows businesses to target specific audiences. Email marketing helps maintain communication with customers. Social media platforms are also integrated into digital strategies. Therefore, digital marketing provides multiple tools for promotion.

The main advantage of digital marketing is its ability to measure results in real time. Businesses can track user behavior and improve their strategies accordingly. However, it also requires technical knowledge and constant updates. Therefore, digital marketing is dynamic and continuously evolving.
