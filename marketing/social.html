Social media marketing involves promoting products and services through platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. It allows businesses to connect directly with their audience and build relationships. Companies create engaging content such as videos, posts, and advertisements to attract customers. Therefore, social media marketing is highly interactive.

Influencers play a major role in social media marketing by promoting products to their followers. This helps brands reach a wider audience. Social media also allows instant feedback from customers, helping businesses improve their services. Therefore, it enhances communication between companies and consumers.

However, competition is high on social media, and maintaining visibility can be challenging. Consistent content and creativity are required for success. Therefore, social media marketing requires careful planning and strategy.
